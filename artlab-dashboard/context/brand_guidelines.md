# Brand Guidelines

## Color Palette
- Primary: Deep purple (#6C5CE7)
- Secondary: Cyan (#00CEC9)
- Accent: Gold (#FDCB6E)

## Typography
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Monospace: JetBrains Mono

## Mood
- Futuristic but warm
- Professional yet creative
- Dark theme with vibrant accents
